# PATROL MANUAL

This is the TBSPedia Online Patrol Manual for the Tahoe Backcountry Ski Patrol. The patrol manual is required reading for current and prospective patrollers. Some material is out of date, please help keep this manual updated!

Recruiting and training are a top priority for TBSP. Refer likely candidate prospects to the recruiter or patrol director at [recruiter@tbsp.org](mailto:recruiter@tbsp.org).

Matters not covered in this manual should be addressed to the Patrol Director or other appropriate patrol officer. Questions and comments are always welcome.

---

### [Chapter 05: Communications](http://tbspweb.tbsp.org/tbspedia/Chapter_05__Communications.html)
